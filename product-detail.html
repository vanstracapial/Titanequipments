Overview

The TX-350 Hydraulic Excavator represents the pinnacle of 35-ton class machinery, combining raw power with precision control. Engineered for the most demanding construction, mining, and earthmoving applications, this excavator delivers exceptional performance while maintaining fuel efficiency and operator comfort.

The heart of the TX-350 is the Cummins QSB6.7 Tier 4 Final engine, delivering 268 horsepower with optimized fuel consumption. The advanced electronic control system ensures maximum power output while minimizing emissions and operating costs.

The hydraulic system features load-sensing technology that adjusts pump output to match demand, resulting in smooth, precise control and reduced energy waste. The dual-pump system provides a combined flow of 560 L/min for fast, powerful operation.

Key Features

  • Cummins QSB6.7 Tier 4 Final engine with 268 HP
  • Advanced load-sensing hydraulic system
  • Spacious ROPS-certified operator cabin
  • Intelligent electronic control with diagnostics
  • Reinforced undercarriage for extended service life
