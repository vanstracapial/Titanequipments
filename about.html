About Titan Equipment

Building the future, one machine at a time

Our Story

Two Decades of Excellence

Founded in 2001, Titan Equipment began with a simple mission: provide construction professionals with reliable, high-quality machinery at competitive prices. What started as a small dealership in Houston has grown into a global leader in heavy equipment sales.

Over the past 23 years, we've supplied over 2,800 machines to clients in 52 countries. From small contractors to multinational construction firms, our customers trust us to deliver the equipment that powers their projects.

Our success is built on three core principles: quality products, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service. Every machine we sell undergoes rigorous inspection, and our global logistics network ensures timely delivery to any location.
